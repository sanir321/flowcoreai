Why is this needed?

Ensuring Stability and Account Safety on WhatsApp

🕒 24-Hour Response Window

The Problem: Meta (WhatsApp) strictly forbids AI/automated bots from initiating "out-of-the-blue" messages to customers after 24 hours of silence.

The Consequence: Sending a message after 24 hours without a "Template Message" can lead to your WhatsApp number being **flagged or permanently banned**.

The Solution: We must check the timestamp of the last customer message before every AI reply.

⌨️ Artificial Typing Delay

The Problem: Instant AI replies feel "robotic" and are easy for WhatsApp's anti-spam systems to detect as non-human behavior.

The Consequence: High-frequency, instant replies can trigger automated spam filters.

The Solution: We simulate a human speed (35ms per character) and show a "typing..." status. This makes the interaction feel natural and reduces the risk of bot-detection.

Goal: Make the AI behave like a polite, compliant human operator so your business account stays safe.

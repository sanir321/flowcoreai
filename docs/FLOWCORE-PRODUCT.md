# FlowCore — AI Customer Service Platform

> Never miss a customer message. Automate replies across WhatsApp and webchat with intelligent AI agents.

---

## What is FlowCore?

FlowCore is a multi-channel AI customer service platform designed for small and medium businesses. It connects WhatsApp and webchat into one unified inbox, where AI agents handle customer inquiries automatically — answering questions, booking appointments, capturing leads, and escalating to humans when needed.

No coding. No complex setup. Connect your WhatsApp and start automating in minutes.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Multi-Channel Inbox

- **WhatsApp** — Connect your business WhatsApp number. All messages come into one inbox.
- **Webchat Widget** — Embed a chat widget on your website. Customers can reach you directly from your site.
- **Unified View** — All conversations from every channel appear in a single inbox. No switching between apps.

### 2. AI Agent Team

FlowCore doesn't use a single bot. It uses a **team of specialized AI agents** that work together:

| Agent | What It Does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Customer Support** | Answers business questions, explains policies, looks up information from your knowledge base |
| **Appointment Booker** | Checks availability, books appointments, reschedules or cancels — syncs with Google Calendar |
| **Sales Assistant** | Captures leads, answers pricing questions, qualifies prospects |

Agents automatically hand off to each other when needed. If a customer asks about booking, Customer Support passes them to Appointment Booker seamlessly.

### 3. Knowledge Base

Upload your business documents, FAQs, and policies. The AI automatically searches this knowledge base to answer customer questions accurately.

- Upload PDFs, DOCX, or paste URLs
- Semantic search finds relevant information even with partial queries
- Works across all channels — WhatsApp and webchat

### 4. Smart Routing & Human Escalation

The AI doesn't guess — it routes conversations intelligently:

- **Auto-routing** — Incoming messages are analyzed and sent to the right agent type
- **Team handoff** — Agents transfer conversations to each other when a specialist is needed
- **Human escalation** — When the AI can't help, it escalates to your team with full conversation context
- **No repeating** — The next agent or human sees everything already discussed

### 5. Appointment Booking (Google Calendar)

Customers can book appointments naturally through chat:

- "I'd like to book a haircut tomorrow at 2 PM"
- AI checks availability, confirms details, creates the booking
- Syncs directly with your Google Calendar
- Customer receives confirmation via WhatsApp + email

### 6. Analytics & Insights

Understand your business performance:

- **Dashboard** — Active conversations, response times, messages handled
- **Traffic chart** — See message volume over time
- **CEO Analyst** — Ask business questions in plain English: "How many leads this week?" or "What's our busiest day?"
- **PostHog integration** — Track user behavior and funnel analytics

### 7. Contact Management

Every customer profile is unified across channels:

- See full conversation history for each contact
- Track which channel they use
- Add notes, tags, and custom fields
- View past orders, appointments, and interactions

### 8. Notification System

Stay informed without being overwhelmed:

| Mode | Behavior |
|------|----------|
| **Instant** | Get notified immediately for every new message |
| **Digest** | Receive a daily summary of all conversations |
| **Off** | Handle everything from the inbox at your own pace |

Choose which events trigger notifications: new conversations, escalated chats, or all messages. Notifications can go to email or WhatsApp.

## How It Works

```
Customer sends message
        ↓
WhatsApp or Webchat
        ↓
FlowCore AI receives message
        ↓
Finds relevant info from Knowledge Base
        ↓
AI Agent responds naturally
        ↓
If needed → hands off to specialist or human
        ↓
Customer gets reply in seconds
```

**Example conversation:**

> **Customer:** Hi, do you offer home delivery?  
> **AI:** Yes, we do! We deliver within 5 km of our store, free for orders above ₹500. What would you like to order?  
> **Customer:** Great, I'd like to book a service appointment for Saturday.  
> **AI:** Let me transfer you to our Appointment Booker who can help with scheduling!

## Setup Guide

### Step 1: Create an Account

Sign up at [flowter.app](https://flowter-bay.vercel.app) with your email. Verify your email with the OTP sent to your inbox.

### Step 2: Set Up Your Workspace

- Name your workspace
- Choose your business category
- Accept the terms of service

### Step 3: Connect WhatsApp

- Go to **Settings → WhatsApp Bridge**
- Click **Connect New Device**
- Scan the QR code with your WhatsApp mobile app (Settings → Linked Devices)
- Your WhatsApp is now connected

### Step 4: Configure AI Agents

- Go to **Agent Hub**
- Enable the agents you need (Customer Support, Appointment Booker, Sales)
- Customize each agent's name and instructions
- Set up your knowledge base by uploading documents

### Step 5: Add Your Knowledge Base

- Go to **Knowledge**
- Upload FAQs, service menus, price lists, policies (PDF or DOCX)
- Or paste URLs to import content
- The AI will automatically reference this information

### Step 6: Connect Google Calendar (for bookings)

- Go to **Settings → Integrations**
- Connect your Google account
- FlowCore syncs your calendar for appointment availability

### Step 7: Embed Webchat (optional)

- Go to **Settings → Webchat**
- Copy the widget code
- Paste it into your website's HTML
- Customers can now chat with you from your site

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need technical skills to set this up?**  
A: No. The entire setup is done through the dashboard. Scanning a QR code connects WhatsApp. Uploading documents sets up the knowledge base.

**Q: Which WhatsApp numbers are supported?**  
A: Any personal or business WhatsApp number works. You scan a QR code — no business API approval needed.

**Q: Can I have human agents alongside AI?**  
A: Yes. When the AI can't handle a request, it escalates to your team with full conversation history.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We comply with GDPR guidelines. You can export or delete your data at any time.

**Q: Does the AI sound natural?**  
A: Yes. The AI is trained to write like a friendly human texting on a phone — short messages, natural language, one question at a time.

**Q: Can I customize how the AI responds?**  
A: Absolutely. You can customize agent names, instructions, and tone. The knowledge base content also shapes responses.

**Q: How fast does the AI reply?**  
A: Typically within 2-5 seconds. The AI processes messages in real-time.

**Q: What if the internet goes down?**  
A: FlowCore runs on cloud infrastructure. As long as your WhatsApp device has internet, messages will be processed even if your office internet is down.
